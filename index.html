Genshin Characters List

Kamisato Ayaka

The princess of the Kamisato House. She occasionally practices swordsmanship so that she can pass time by slicing every falling snowflake floating around her on a snowy day.

Ganyu

The secretary at Yuehai Pavilion. The blood of the qilin, an illuminated beast, flows within her veins..

Kaedehara Kazuha

A wandering samurai of the once-famed Kaedehara Clan, Kazuha is a temporary crewmember of The Crux after being declared a criminal by Baal for taking a Vision from her grasp.

Xiao

One of the mighty and illuminated adepti guarding Liyue, also heralded as the "Vigilant Yaksha." Despite his youthful appearance, tales of his exploits have been documented for millennia.
